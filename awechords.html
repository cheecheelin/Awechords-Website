Hello!


We are a co-ed cappella group at UCLA that strives to share our passion for music with the LA community and empower our members' musicality. We started in 2006 as 'Nhip Am', a choir for the annual Vietnamese Culture Night. Since then, we have expanded to more diverse audiences throughout Los Angeles and in regrouped in 2010 to become Awechords.

Our mission: to learn, love, and live music and to bond with each other in the process. We welcome both North & South Campus majors, international and domestic students to be part of our family.

Our style: we've come to sing a variety of genres and songs, all the way from Justin Timberlake, Sara Bareilles, Coldplay, and One Republic, to some oldies such as Toto and even broadway shows such as Wicked.

How do I join?


The audition process will be around 10 minutes long and will include scales, sight reading, tonal memory, and one to two solo songs of your choice. The solos should be approximately 30 seconds (a verse and a chorus) and be of different styles. Also, if you know how to beatbox, definitely audition for that as well! Don't worry, just be yourself. We don't bite!
